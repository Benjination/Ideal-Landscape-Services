Just wanted to send you a note to tell you in writing how pleased our family is with our back yard renovation. It is nothing of spectacular. Your team has given us back this space and made it functional, beautiful and safe.
Please feel free to use this letter as a recommendation to your future clients. I would be pleased to show anyone your handiwork and to discuss your business practices. You have earned a customer for life.
