David Felker, President
David is a dedicated family man with a passion for business and the great outdoors. He began serving his lawn care clients in 1979, and has over the years nurtured Ideal Landscape Services, Inc. into a successful landscaping and lawn maintenance vehicle for transforming your ideas into reality. With over 35 years’ experience in construction, maintenance and installation, David has gained expertise in balance, placement, irrigation and xeriscape creating thriving southern landscapes that bring satisfaction to our clients year after year.
Ericka Felker, Vice President
Behind every good man, there is a good lady. Ericka brings elements of outreach and customer care to our clients by keeping in touch with who we are at the core. Her love for people and desire to deliver the best customer service possible is the key that we attribute to client retention. Ericka’s knowledge of social media resources keeps us connected with the people we serve on a more intimate and personal level.
Kathy Pinke, Office Manager
With a firm foundation in southern hospitality, Kathy truly believes “The customer is ALWAYS right!” First and foremost is her desire to deliver a friendly, peaceful and personal atmosphere for whoever may be calling! For over 10 years, Kathy has developed relationships with our clients and, often times, knows who is calling just by the sound of their voices. As a single mom, Kathy prides herself in her ability to handle the demands raising her family and running a successful business that benefits the community and the families it encompasses.
Our Crew, The Backbone
We proudly employ some of the most humble and hardworking family men, who take pride in what they do day after day. Most of our employees have worked for us a minimum of 10 – 15 years, faithfully. Our guys are honest, hardworking men who go the extra mile to take care of their clients’ homes as if they were their own. Ideal Landscapes would not be what it is today without our guys!
