Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Ideal Landscape Services ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form or request a quote on our website, we collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your street address, city, and ZIP code
  • Project details and preferences you provide
  • Any additional comments or questions you include

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes
  • Schedule consultations and coordinate project work
  • Communicate with you about your landscaping project

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase to securely store contact form submissions. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Authorize.Net: Payment processing for invoices is handled by Authorize.Net. We do not store credit card information on our servers. Authorize.Net's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
