Landscape Maintenance

There is a significant difference between hiring a "lawn maintenance" company and having professionals who genuinely understand living plants and holistic landscape care. Ideal Landscape Services provides professional care that keeps every element of your property looking its absolute best, season after season.

Our maintenance programs are designed around your specific property. We evaluate the existing plants, hardscapes, drainage, and irrigation to establish an appropriate schedule. Unlike most maintenance companies, we also attend to non-living elements — loose bricks, trim pieces, and small structural concerns that can affect the overall appearance and safety of your property.

What We Maintain

  • Seasonal planting, pruning, and fertilization programs
  • Shrub shaping and bed clean-up
  • Mulch, stone, and ground cover refreshes
  • Irrigation inspection and adjustment recommendations
  • Drainage maintenance — dry creek beds, French drains, grading
  • Pest and disease monitoring with professional referrals
  • Non-living elements: loose retaining wall bricks, trim, lighting fixtures
  • Commercial maintenance — entries, signage areas, parking landscapes

A well-maintained landscape keeps your investment vibrant, safe, and attractive. For commercial clients, we also ensure that signage is never obscured by overgrowth and that first impressions remain strong at every visit. Whether you have one of our original Ideal designs or an existing landscape in need of professional care, we have a maintenance plan for you.
