Landscape Lighting

Landscape lighting is a great way to enhance the appearance of your home at night while also increasing security and safety in and around your property. Your home is the largest investment most people make — the added landscaping investment should be enjoyed no matter the time of day or night.

The latest advances in low-voltage, LED lighting allow significant impact while reducing energy costs. Ideal Landscape Services uses environmentally friendly lighting solutions for outdoor walkways, trees, patios, decks, and more. Landscape lighting adds another layer of depth to any landscape design and brings it to life after dark.

Lighting Benefits

  • Enhanced beauty — highlight the best features of your home and landscape
  • Increased security — a significant deterrent to potential burglars
  • Improved safety — illuminated walkways for guests arriving after dark
  • Extended usability of patios and decks after sunset
  • Low-voltage, LED technology — minimal impact on your electric bill
  • Increases property value

Our design team is ready to work with you to determine which areas to highlight and with which fixtures. We'll outline which elements of the plan are more for aesthetic reasons and which have added safety and security benefits.

In the Texas heat, it can sometimes be challenging to enjoy the outdoors during the day. In the evening, once the temperature has dropped, proper lighting means the party can begin. Let us help you maximize your outdoor spaces after dark.
