About Ideal Landscape

Our Story

Serving DFW Since 1979

Ideal Landscape Services is a family-owned company that has been creating beautiful, lasting outdoor spaces across the Dallas–Fort Worth Metroplex for over 45 years. What started as a single man's passion for exceptional outdoor design has grown into one of the area's most trusted names in landscape design, water features, hardscapes, outdoor living, and maintenance.

We believe every property deserves a landscape that enhances life — not just curb appeal. From intimate residential gardens to large commercial campuses, we bring the same level of personal care and craftsmanship to every project we take on.

We are proud members of the Texas Nursery & Landscape Association (TNLA) and have received recognition at the Fort Worth Home and Garden Show for outstanding landscape design.

The People

Meet the Team

Every project benefits from the care, experience, and personal touch of our people. From the first phone call to the finished installation, you'll work with professionals who take your home seriously.

David Felker

President & Founder

A family man with a lifelong passion for landscape artistry, David has been serving clients since 1979. With over 45 years of experience, he brings unmatched expertise in site balance, plant placement, irrigation, and xeriscape design. Every project benefits from his hands-on attention and genuine care for each client's property.

Ericka Felker

Vice President

Ericka leads customer care, client outreach, and Ideal's social media presence. She is the voice you often hear on the phone and the face behind the accounts that showcase the team's best work. Ericka's dedication to client relationships is a key reason customers keep coming back year after year.

Kathy Pinke

Office Manager

Kathy has been with Ideal for over 10 years and runs the office with warmth and southern hospitality. She knows most clients by the sound of their voice. A dedicated single mother, Kathy brings exceptional care and organization to every call, invoice, and appointment — keeping everything running smoothly behind the scenes.

In the Field

Our Crew — The Backbone

The real magic of Ideal Landscape Services happens in the field, where our talented crew brings every design to life. These are humble, hardworking family men who have been with us for 10 to 15 years. They treat every client's home the way they'd want their own treated.

Their level of care is rare. They know our standards, our clients, and our commitment to quality — and they embody it every single day. No project gets handed off to a subcontractor. When you hire Ideal, you get us.
